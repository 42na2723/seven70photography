About JoJo

Seven70 Photography is the creative home of JoJo Tallorin, a Melbourne-based photographer with a strong passion for motorsport, events, people, travel, and storytelling.

My Story

I capture moments with energy, emotion, and purpose. From high-speed motorsport action to quiet candid details, my goal is to create images that feel alive and memorable.

Seven70 Style

Bold contrast, cinematic colour, clean composition, and attention to the decisive moment. Every frame is created to tell a story and preserve the atmosphere of the day.

Based In Melbourne

Available for motorsport, events, portraits, travel projects, and selected creative collaborations across Victoria and beyond.
