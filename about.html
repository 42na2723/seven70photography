About the photographer

Capturing the moment. Preserving the story.

Hi, I’m JoJo Tallorin, the photographer behind Seven70 Photography. Based in Australia, I created Seven70 as a place to share my passion for motorsport, events, portraits, street photography, travel and creative storytelling.

My photography is driven by emotion, movement and atmosphere. Whether I’m shooting trackside at a motorsport event, documenting people and places, or capturing quiet details behind the scenes, my goal is always the same: create images that feel alive and memorable.

Motorsport has a special place in my work. I love the speed, sound, discipline and character surrounding every event — from the action on track to the dedication of drivers, crews and organisers behind it.

My Style

Cinematic, bold and story-driven — with attention to light, timing, composition and emotion.

What I Photograph

Motorsport, events, portraits, street, travel, behind-the-scenes and creative documentary moments.

My Philosophy

A photograph should make you feel something. It should preserve not only what happened, but how the moment felt.

Let’s create something memorable.

If you’re looking for motorsport coverage, event photography, portraits or creative visual storytelling, I’d love to help bring your vision to life.
