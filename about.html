About the photographer

Capturing the moment. Preserving the story.

Hi, I’m JoJo Tallorin, the photographer behind Seven70 Photography. Based in Australia, I created Seven70 as a place to share my passion for motorsport, events, street photography, travel, nature, wildlife, portraits and creative storytelling.

My photography is driven by emotion, movement and atmosphere. Whether I’m standing trackside at a motorsport event, documenting people and places, or capturing quiet details behind the scenes, my goal is always the same: create images that feel alive, honest and memorable.

Motorsport has a special place in my work. I love the speed, sound, discipline and character surrounding every event — from the action on track to the dedication of drivers, crews and organisers behind it.

My Style

My style is cinematic, bold and story-driven. I look for strong light, clean composition, emotion and timing — the split-second details that make an image feel alive.

I enjoy images with atmosphere: motion blur, dramatic contrast, natural beauty, real expressions and scenes that feel authentic rather than staged.

What I Photograph

I photograph motorsport, events, street, travel, nature, wildlife and portraits.

From race cars at speed to wildlife, landscapes, candid street moments and portraits, my focus is always the same: capture the energy, emotion and detail that make each moment unique.

My Philosophy

Photography, for me, is more than taking pictures. It is about telling stories and preserving moments that people can feel long after the moment has passed.

I believe the best photographs come from being present, patient and connected to what is happening in front of you — whether that moment is full of speed, silence, movement or emotion.
